Origin Story

From Zero
To Le Mans

"Once in a decade talent who refused to let circumstances define his ceiling."

Sebastian Moreno's journey began on a karting track in Colombia — no factory support, no wealthy family backing, just a boy with an obsession for speed and the will to outwork everyone around him. He climbed through every category available in South America on pure merit, eventually opening his own door to Europe's most prestigious endurance platform: the Michelin Le Mans Cup.

The Return

Against
All Odds

A serious accident threatened to end Sebastian's career before its European chapter could begin. Facing a recovery that would have broken most, he chose a different path — staying inside the sport as a coach, mentor, and strategist while rebuilding physically and mentally.

That time inside the cockpit of knowledge, rather than the cockpit of a racing car, transformed him. When he returned to competition, he was not the same driver. He was better.
